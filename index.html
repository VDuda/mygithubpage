Unfortunately, Konsul is no longer operating.

We tried everything in our power and worked relentlessly to try to find an alternative path forward, but unfortunately, it came down to this. As much as we wanted Konsul to continue to support candidates from diverse backgrounds, because of the market shifts caused by COVID-19, we had to make this decision.

That being said, we’re proud to say that throughout Konsul’s history, we’ve awarded ~$80,000 of prizes to help people pay for college and pay off their student loan debt. We’ve also helped a number of candidates land jobs at great tech companies.

We’re truly grateful for all the people who supported us throughout this journey.

If you have any questions or you’re trying to reach someone, please email founders@konsul.com or contact them directly on LinkedIn.
